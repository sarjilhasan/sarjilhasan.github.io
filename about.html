How To

To convert a binary (base 2) number to a decimal (base 10), you will need to:

  1. Count how many digits there are from right to left (from 0) and write it in the bottom.
  2. Look at every '1' with the place holder you put from counting how many digits there were.
  3. Now take the '1' and multiply it by '2' to the place holder it falls into.

    Ex. 1 x 2^4, where '4' is the 4th digit of the number's length.

  4. We do not care about the '0's because anything times '0' is just '0'.
  5. After you have times everything by '2', add up all the results.
  6. CONGRATS! You just converted your first binary number to a decimal.
